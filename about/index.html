About

I grew up in India and did my undergraduate studies in engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Varanasi. I am interested in pursuing academic research in the field of development economics, public economics, international trade, inequality and long run growth. Prior to starting at LSE, I worked for two years each at the Jameel Poverty Action Lab in India, and the Energy Policy Institute at University of Chicago.
